The Project
Context and
motivation
⚡ The Problem

English grammar is often taught through repetitive, static exercises that generate low motivation in students. The lack of immediate feedback and the absence of game-like elements limit the retention of key concepts.

✅ The Solution: Gamification

GRAMMARX transforms grammar practice into a competitive and collaborative real-time experience, where speed and accuracy are both rewarded.

→Instant feedback per answer (correct / wrong)
→Configurable time pressure that boosts concentration
→Friendly competition between peers in the same classroom
→100% random questions — every game is unique
👥 Target audience
📚English students at A2–B2 level (CEFR)
🏫English teachers in in-person or hybrid environments
💻Continuing education courses and self-study learners
🎓 Grammar content
⏰Past Simple — regular & irregular verbs, sentence order
⚔️Comparatives & Superlatives — formation rules, exceptions
🔍Present Perfect — have/has + past participle, reading comprehension
🔮Future Tense — will vs. going to, predictions and plans
Game modes
GRAMMARX
The 4 game modes
⏰
Time Machine
Past Simple · Syntax
The words of a Past Simple sentence are shuffled. The player must tap the words in the correct order to reconstruct the sentence before time runs out.
Regular verbs Irregular verbs Word order
⚔️
Duel Mode
Comparatives & Superlatives
Four answer choices appear on screen. The player selects the correct comparative or superlative form of the given adjective, applying the rules for short and long adjectives.
ADJer than the ADJest more / most ADJ
🔍
Evidence File
Present Perfect · Comprehension
A short professional-context passage is shown. The player reads it and answers a comprehension question related to completed actions using the Present Perfect.
have / has + PP Comprehension Work context
🔮
Crystal Ball
Future Tense · Selection
An incomplete sentence with a blank is presented. The player selects the correct future form from four options: will, going to, won't, or other variants.
will / won't going to Predictions
