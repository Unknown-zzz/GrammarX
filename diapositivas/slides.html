The Challenge

Why does grammar
feel boring?

😴 Passive, repetitive exercises — students complete them mechanically with no real engagement or motivation to improve.
⏳ No immediate feedback — by the time a student knows they got something wrong, the lesson has already moved on.
🏝️ Students work in isolation — there is no competitive element that creates urgency, attention, or memory retention.
"Students don't need more grammar worksheets — they need a reason to care."
Our Answer

Introducing GRAMMARX

Gamification meets English grammar practice.
🏆Competitive real-time matches — the whole class plays simultaneously, racing for the top spot on the podium.
⚡Instant feedback per answer — correct or wrong shown immediately, with a speed bonus that rewards fast thinking.
🎮Teacher controls the room — host selects game mode, time limit, and advances each round from their dashboard.
🔀Always unique sessions — questions are randomly drawn from a bank of 40+ per mode across 5 difficulty levels.
Kahoot-style No sign-up Any device Built from scratch
Game Modes

The 4 Mini-Games

Each mode targets a different grammar structure · 5 difficulty levels each
⏰
Time Machine
Past Simple · Word Order
Words of a Past Simple sentence are scrambled. Tap them in the correct order before time runs out.
⚔️
Duel Mode
Comparatives & Superlatives
Two things are compared. Choose the correct comparative or superlative form from 4 options.
🔍
Evidence File
Present Perfect · Error Detection
A Present Perfect sentence with one grammar error is shown. Find and correct it from 4 options.
🔮
Crystal Ball
Future Tense
Read the context. Choose between will, going to, or won't to complete the sentence correctly.
Game Flow

How to Play

No installation · No sign-up · Just a browser
①
Teacher creates a room
A 6-character code + QR code are generated automatically — project them on the board.
②
Students join from any device
Open grammarx.onrender.com · Enter name · Type the room code or scan the QR.
③
Host picks game mode & time per round
Choose one of 4 mini-games · Set timer (10s – 60s) · Everyone reads instructions · Press ▶ START.
④
10 rounds — fastest + most accurate wins
Points = base score + speed bonus · Podium shown at the end · Play again instantly.
